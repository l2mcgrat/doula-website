About

Hi, I’m Camille

I’m a young, well-read, and deeply passionate doula who brings calm, steady support to the most transformative season of your life. My approach is evidence-informed and heart-led: I blend research, practical tools, and a grounded presence so you feel prepared, heard, and confident in your choices. I’m here to offer reassurance, advocacy, and gentle guidance—before, during, and after birth—so you never feel like you have to do this alone.

My Philosophy

  • Your body, your baby, your choices. I support informed decision-making without pressure or judgement.
  • Calm changes everything. I help create a steady, grounded environment where you can breathe and focus.
  • Evidence + intuition can co-exist. We use research, your lived experience, and what feels right for you.
  • Postpartum matters. I support rest, nourishment, and realistic rhythms—because recovery is part of the birth story.
