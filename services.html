Services

Birth support, postpartum care, packages, and pricing.

✧ Birth Support

Continuous presence and advocacy through labor and birth.

  • Prenatal Visits — One-on-one meetings to discuss your birth preferences, anxieties, and hopes. We explore comfort measures, positions, and what support means to you.
  • On-Call Window — I'm available from your estimated due date through labor onset, ready to respond to your needs in real time.
  • Labor Support — Continuous physical, emotional, and informational support throughout your labor. I work alongside your care team and partner.
  • Post-Birth Follow-Up — Immediate support after birth, gentle check-in within 48 hours, and resources for your early days.

✧ Postpartum Care

Tender, practical support during your fourth trimester recovery.

  • Emotional Support — A steady, compassionate presence to listen, validate, and support you through the emotional landscape of early parenthood.
  • Newborn Care Guidance — Practical help with feeding, sleep, diaper changes, and gentle observation to help you build confidence.
  • Light Household Support — Meal prep, tidying, laundry, and errands so you can focus on rest and bonding with your baby.
  • Resources + Referrals — Connections to lactation support, mental health professionals, and community resources tailored to your needs.

✧ Workshops

Community, education, and celebration around birth and parenthood.

  • Circles & Community Support — Gather with other pregnant folks or postpartum parents for shared experience, grounding practices, and collective wisdom.
  • Education and Preparation — Workshops on comfort measures, labor coping, feeding, postpartum recovery, and partner involvement tailored to your needs.
  • Special Events — Seasonal circles, sister-hood celebrations, and community gatherings that honor the sacredness of birth and early parenthood.
